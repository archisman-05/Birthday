Dear Bhoomi Bose,

I didn’t know then what you would come to mean to me, how you would become the most important person in my life. But now, I can’t imagine a day without you in my thoughts, without feeling your presence even when you’re not beside me.

I crave to see those beautiful eyes, the ones that speak to me without a single word. I long to lose myself in them, to find my world in their depth. Your lips, the way they curve into a smile, the way they whisper my name—it’s a melody I want to hear for the rest of my life. And the scent of your hair, that soft, intoxicating fragrance, it lingers in my mind, wrapping around me like a memory I never want to fade.

Today, on your special day, I wish you the happiest birthday, my love. I wish for you all the happiness in the world, because you deserve nothing less. I want you to always know how incredibly loved you are, how cherished, how irreplaceable. No matter where life takes us, I promise you this—I will be with you till the very end. Through every joy and every sorrow, through every sunrise and every storm, my hand will always be reaching for yours.

You are my love, my mistu, my home, my forever. And I am yours. Happy Birthday, my love.

With warmest wishes,
Archisman
